About us

Discover Our Story

Simplifying Student Housing in Phnom Penh

We started HomMi because we know how difficult and stressful it can be for students to find a safe, affordable, and comfortable room in Phnom Penh. Our mission is to fix that. We are a digital-first platform that connects students directly with trusted rooms near their universities. You can easily filter by your locations, prices and facilities, so you can find your perfect new home with confidence.

  • Find all the best rooms in one place and save time.
  • We verify every listing for your safety and peace of mind.
  • We only list practical and affordable rooms perfect for students.
